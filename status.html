📦 Shipping & 🔄 Return Policy

1. Order Processing & Dispatch

  • All confirmed orders placed before 6:00 PM are processed and dispatched the same business day.
  • Orders placed after 6:00 PM will be dispatched on the next business day.

2. Delivery Timeline

  • Estimated delivery time is 3–7 business days, depending on the delivery location and courier service efficiency.
  • Customers will be notified with tracking details once the order has been dispatched.

3. Pre-Order Policy

  • A 50% advance payment is required to confirm any pre-order.

Important Note:

  • Once a pre-order is confirmed, it cannot be cancelled, refunded, or placed on hold.
  • Customers are strongly advised to verify the product at the time of delivery in the presence of the delivery agent.

4. Return Policy

Eligibility Window
  • Items can be returned within 7 days of purchase.
  • The 7-day period will be counted from the date of delivery.
Condition of Returned Items
  • Returned products must be in their original condition, unused, and with all packaging and accessories intact.
  • Items not meeting these conditions may be declined for return.
Valid Reasons for Return
  • Change of preference is not accepted – Returns cannot be made on grounds such as “I didn’t like the product” or “changed my mind”.
  • Defective on Arrival – Products may only be returned if they are found to be defective or damaged upon delivery.
  • Pre-Ordered Products – Confirmed pre-orders cannot be returned, except in cases where the product is defective on arrival.
Exclusions
  • Pre-order items are not eligible for cancellation, return, or refund (except under the Defective on Arrival clause).
  • Hygiene-sensitive or custom-made products (if applicable) are non-returnable.
